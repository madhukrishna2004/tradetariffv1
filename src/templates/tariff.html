Use the TradeSphere Global lookup tool to classify your goods and find the appropriate HS/commodity code for UK imports and exports.

This tool helps you:

  • Determine applicable customs duties and VAT
  • Identify any tariff suspensions or reductions
  • Understand rules of origin and UKGT rates

To classify goods accurately, gather product details like:

  • Product category and function
  • Material composition
  • Usage and purpose
  • Manufacturing method
  • Packaging or presentation style

Note: UKGT classifications are based on 8-digit codes. If you have a 10-digit code, use only the first 8 digits.
